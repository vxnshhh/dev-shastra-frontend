Dev Shastra .

India's Fast Growing Student Technical Community. We at Dev Shastra plan to give students a chance to perform research and make projects in the areas of AI, ML, DL, Web Development and Android and iOS Development..

What Do We Do ?

We organize Events, Hackathons, and Workshops on different Technologies. Also, We Share Resources, Roadmaps to learn different technologies and share opportunities related to Internships and different tech programs.

The AIM !

We strive to cultivate a change in the current learning strategies of students and incorporate a reasonable methodology to make our interns future prepared with a good knowledge of industrial experience by making them create projects in their respective fields. Also, we intend to expand our ventures to other technical and non-technical fields as well. We believe that you will appreciate the learning excursion and shape your future with us at Dev Shastra.
